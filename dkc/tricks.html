Item Caches

Caches with Donkey

Item caches in DKC require players to use Gorilla Slap on top of them with Donkey or land on top of them while rolling in the air with Diddy. Donkey can't do the latter in most cases due to his hitbox being a few pixels above his ground collision point of reference (a bright point near the bounding box around the kongs):

Despite this, Donkey can still trigger a few caches that are placed near ledges or high places.
