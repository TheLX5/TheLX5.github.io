Archipelago Randomizers

Archipelago is a system that allows games to be randomized in a multiworld setting. I've done quite a few of those already, feel free to look around them and give them a go :P

SMW: Spicy Mycena Waffles

Explore SMW with a twist! You've been stripped out of basic abilities which can be learned again by finding items in the multiworld.

Make your way into Bowser's hideout and beat him once again... or go into a scavenge hunt for Yoshi Eggs scattered all around Dinosaur Land!

This is a fork of the AP SMW Randomizer.

Mega Man X

A powerful maverick emerges, it's up to X to defeat him in a 1v1 combat!

Blast across stages finding upgrades in unusual places, some items might even be inside health pickups or even worse, a random Maverick might have those in their filthy hands!

There may be other new upgrades from future entries here...

Mega Man X2

Sigma's back with another evil plan!

Like in the previous adventure, go through stages after finding their coordinates and seek help anywhere you're able to, don't forget to check inside small health pickups!

Mega Man X3

Sigma's back AGAIN!

Another adventure, another set of levels to play and upgrades to find!

Thinking about it, all three MMX games share the same randomizer design philosophy... so have fun like in X and X2! :D

Donkey Kong Country

K. Rool managed to snatch Donkey Kong's Banana Hoard, abilities, animal buddies and some objects!

Find every Big Banana to get clues about K. Rool's whereabouts and get back your Banana Hoard to teach him a lesson to not to mess with one's bananas.

DKC Mirror

It's the same thing as DKC but everything is mirrored!

Find every Big Banana to get clues about K. Rool's whereabouts and get back your Banana Hoard to teach him a lesson to not to mess with one's bananas... while flipped horizontally.

Donkey Kong Country 2

K. Rool learned from Bowser in SMW and stripped Diddy and Dixie from their abilities, animal buddies and even barrels!?

Play levels, find secrets and even collect KONG letters to learn back your abilities! K. Rool must be taught a lesson once again to learn to not mess with the Kong family.
